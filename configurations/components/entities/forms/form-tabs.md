# Form Tabs

When Adding a Form Tab, you need have your form created and saved, therefore you will click on <mark style="color:$warning;">**#1**</mark>**&#x20;Form Tabs** tab. If you have Tabs existing for the specific form, they will then be displayed in a grid form in the **Form tabs** section. Therefore click on the <mark style="color:$warning;">**#2**</mark> **Add new** Button.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/1 (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The form to add a Form tab will then be displayed, where the field in <mark style="color:$warning;">**#1**</mark> is the Form tab **Display Name**, <mark style="color:$warning;">**#2**</mark> is the **Unique Name** of the Button you are creating, <mark style="color:$warning;">**#3**</mark>  is the **sort Index** which allows you to place your Form tabs in a specific order therefore, On the <mark style="color:$warning;">**#4**</mark> field is the prepopulated Form name, which you are trying to add the form tab in.
