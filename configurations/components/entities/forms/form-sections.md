# Form Sections

Form sections allow you to divide your form into sections of choice, where you can add different form types such as your normal Form with fields, **Sub-grids** and more.

When Adding a Form section you need have your form created and saved, therefore you will click on <mark style="color:$warning;">**#1**</mark>**&#x20;Form Sections** tab. If you have Sections existing for the specific form, they will then be displayed in a grid form in the **Form Sections** section. Therefore click on the <mark style="color:$warning;">**#2**</mark> **Add new** Button.
